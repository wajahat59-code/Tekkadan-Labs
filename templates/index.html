Explore Our Fields

AI & Machine Learning

  • How AI works (neural networks, decision trees, NLP, CV)
  • Training and evaluating ML models
  • Using Python libraries like Scikit-Learn, TensorFlow, PyTorch
  • Building real projects — prediction models, chatbots, classifiers

Game Design & Simulations

  • Learn principles of game mechanics, player psychology, and level design
  • Build interactive prototypes using Unity, Godot, or similar engines
  • Implement game physics, AI agents, and simulation loops
  • Create and iterate on UX, visual storytelling, and balancing systems

Space & Astronomy

  • Understand celestial mechanics, orbital dynamics, and basic astrophysics
  • Learn observational techniques: telescopes, imaging, and data reduction
  • Explore planetology, stellar evolution, and cosmology fundamentals
  • Work with real astronomical datasets and simulation tools

Data Science & Statistics

  • Master descriptive and inferential statistics for real data problems
  • Clean, visualize, and explore datasets using Python
  • Translate data insights into actionable recommendations and dashboards
  • Understand experiment design, A/B testing, and reproducible workflows

Theoretical & Applied Physics

  • Develop strong foundations in classical mechanics, electromagnetism, and quantum basics
  • Apply mathematical methods to model physical systems
  • Use simulation tools for complex systems and experimental design
  • Gain problem-solving habits transferable to engineering and research projects

Environmental & Earth Sciences

  • Learn ecosystem structure, biogeochemical cycles, and Earth system interactions
  • Study climate science, weather patterns, and anthropogenic impacts
  • Collect and analyze field and remote-sensing data (GIS basics)
  • Design sustainable solutions and evaluate environmental risks

Digital Ethics

  • Explore privacy, fairness, transparency, and accountability in tech
  • Learn frameworks for ethical design and responsible AI development
  • Analyze real case studies of bias, surveillance, and consent
  • Practice translating ethical principles into product decisions and policy proposals

Cognitive, Social & Behavioral Psychology

  • Understand cognitive processes: attention, memory, learning, and decision-making
  • Study social influences, group dynamics, and interpersonal behavior
  • Design and run behavioral experiments and surveys; analyze results statistically
  • Apply psychological principles to UX, education, and human-centered design
